Get Dirty

Louisville has a great mountain bike scene. There are a number of trails in the area that are a short drive.

Trails include Cherokee Park, Waverly Park, Turkey Run.

If you want to travel a little further there is O'bannon Woods in Cordyon, Capital View Park in Frankfort and Brown County State Park in Nashville, IN.

Microbrews!

After a long ride nothing is better than a cold frosty. Fortunately Louisville has plenty of options.

Choose from great breweries such as Against the Grain, Great Flood, BBC, Gravely, and many more.
